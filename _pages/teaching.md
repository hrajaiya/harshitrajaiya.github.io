# University of Ottawa

**Financial Management (Undergraduate)**  
Core course introducing financial analysis, valuation, and investment decision-making.  

---

**Recent Developments in Finance Research (PhD)**  
Doctoral seminar discussing frontier research papers in corporate finance, entrepreneurial finance, fintech, among other topics.  

---

**Advanced Corporate Finance (MSc)**  
Graduate-level course on advanced corporate finance, which covers topics such as innovation, corporate social responsibilities, text analaysis, IPOs, among other topics .
